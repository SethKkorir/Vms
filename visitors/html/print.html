In Case of Fire

**Important:** Familiarize yourself with the fire escape plan before entering the building.

**In the event of a fire:**

  • Activate the nearest fire alarm.
  • Evacuate the building immediately using the designated fire exits.
  • Do not use elevators. Use stairs only.
  • Proceed to the designated meeting point outside the building.
  • Do not re-enter the building until an authorized official gives the all-clear.
